Watch‑Face Overview

Check the Bitcoin price without pulling out your phone. Because that’s what Bitcoiners do.

Features That Actually Matter

  • Real‑Time Price Updates – Auto‑updates every 15/30/60 min (choose the interval)
  • 24 h change % with green ▲ or red ▼ (not financial advice)
  • Dynamic text sizing – ready for Bitcoin at $1 M+
  • Analog or Digital clock modes
  • 5 clock‑hand colors (including iconic Bitcoin Orange #F7931A)
  • 4 background patterns (Solid Black, Diagonal Dots, Hexagon Grid, Flowing Waves)
  • 40 possible combinations
  • Lightweight (1.5 MB) & battery‑efficient
  • Works on 78+ Garmin devices – zero data collection

How to Install

Open Garmin Connect → Device Settings → Watch Faces → Bitcoin Price Watch. Then customize clock style, colors, background patterns, and update frequency.

Compatibility

Venu • Forerunner • Fenix • epix • Vivoactive • MARQ • Approach • Descent (78+ devices supported)
